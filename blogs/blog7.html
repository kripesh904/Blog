CRUSHED

Kripesh Raj Sharma

I'm surrounded by these walls

They are far beyond my height

and my wit

Far beyond my luck and capability

Far beyond me and my voice

Far beyond my life and my dream.

On those floor, me,

looking at these walls high

Pondering

Dreaming

No thoughts cross my mind

These walls look towards me

When I'm writing these words

These words mean nothing to me

Just words on the screen of my phone

I blame myself

For lack of better words

The walls still surrounding me

The wall, I fell, inching closer to me

One second. one word. one inch.

I

I wrote

Am

I wrote

Alive

I wrote

The walls taunting me like this is an irony

Closing its way on me

Hustling my way to close my notebook

“It was a phone”

My way to close my phone and escape

Walls still staring at me

With the look of superiority

Looking at me

Like I'm naive

I thought I could escape from it

I run my mind again and again

The walls closer to me

I open my phone

To write these words to end

I can't escape

I am dead.

Acceptance.
