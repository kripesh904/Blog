Stuck

Kripesh Raj Sharma

You are a brighter sun than the sun
A holier human than the thou
More vibrant than colors,
Livelier than the living,
As strong as a bull,
As weak as a glass.

Painted my life with your memories,
Stained my hands with love.
The only good thing about this morning
is, It ends up in your thoughts.

You are all I wanted,
All I wanted to see,
All I wanted to feel
And I fell, fell in this void of helplessness.
I know, I am the creator of this void,
But once you’re stuck,
You’re stuck.
