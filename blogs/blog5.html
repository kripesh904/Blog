Fake Poet

Kripesh Raj Sharma

I'm no Rumi,

Homer,

Shakespeare,

Or even a poet.

Fear engulfs me,

what would happen

if my pen has no ink left and

my mind devoid of poetry.

Heck it's not poetry.

These words,

are just me

screaming

the world, my thoughts.

My emotions,

these words

speak of my existence.

I write these words,

end with some rhymes,

In hopes they'll speak to me,

That you matter.

And to the world,

I exist.

These words

are a way to find

myself

in the crowd

In those crowds of thousands,

Unknown.

In that crowd
