That Night

Kripesh Raj Sharma

Sometimes,
through the darkness of time.
In sometimes peaceful,
but mostly,
numbing silence.
I pause.
pause to wonder
unwillingly
this unseeable darkness.
this unhearable silence.
after every silent sound,
after every darker sight,
something throws me a question.

what if
this darkness
prevails
through every circle of time,
what if
this silence
continues
till I find myself in a pyre.
what if
this darkness
darkens
the corners of my soul,
what if
this silence
silences
the words of my heart.

To wonder,
the mysteries
and mystics
of this
unseeable darkness
and this
unhearable silence.

And there...

I stay paused,

frustrated.


blind.

and

deaf.
